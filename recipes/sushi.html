Sushi

Description

This recipe shows you how to make delicious homemade sushi, containing salmon avocado and cucumber!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Sushi rice (14 ounces/400 grams)
  • 7 ounces Smoked Salmon
  • 1 Large Ripe Avocado
  • 1 Small Cucumber
  • 5 Nori Sheets
  • 1/4 cup Rice Vinegar (60 ml)
  • 1 tablespoon Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the rice following packet instructions. In a small bowl, combine vinegar, sugar and salt and mix until the sugar is dissolved. Pour this over cooked rice and mix well. Let the rice cool down.
  2. In the meantime, cut the veggies into thin strips. I like cutting the cucumber and carrots into matchsticks, avocado and salmon into chunks (all of them about 4 inch/10 cm long).
  3. Now place mat onto your worktop (see note 2), top it with nori sheet so that the rough side is facing you. Spread the rice over the nori sheet, (see note 3) covering it evenly but leaving about 0.5 inch (1.5 cm) of the sheet free. The rice-free end should be farther from you.
  4. Place a thin row of salmon, cucumber (or carrot) and avocado in the middle of the rice.
  5. Wet the rice-free end with water and roll everything tightly (the mat helps you get a nice round shape and also by applying pressure when rolling, you are making sure the roll stays tight).
  6. Slice and serve!
